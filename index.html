ABOUT

Orrey, the Tech Fest of Miranda House is a commemorative event to celebrate technology. Organised every year by the Computer Science department of Miranda House, the fest showcases, and acknowledges innovations and forthcoming opportunities in the field. Orrey 2016 will be, as per tradition, an exhibition of plethora of young talent and enthusiasm in technology along with being a festival filled with fun and excitement. The theme for Orrey 2016 is “Start Up. Stand Up” in sync with the Start- up India Scheme initiated by the Indian Government. The theme reflects the existing parallel interdependence of the various environments that exist in the technological world and the need to integrate these even further to improve the prevailing conditions in India. Through this theme, we have tried to develop an insight into the real world situation which includes a say from the political, technological, business and social environment.

TIDBITS

“My minds rebels at stagnation. I crave for mental exaltation” ­ Sherlock Holmes. If this quote describes your frame of mind then TIDBITS will surely be your ultimate event destination. With a total of three enthralling rounds sure to test the ability of every nerve cell in your brain, it will also put on trial your team work. A maximum of four members per team are allowed and entry in the next round will be granted only if you are able to crack the previous one. The team of prodigies which is able to make it to the end will be rewarded aptly. This is an event which you will regret missing.

START­UP PITCH

“Build something 100 people love and not something one million people kind of like” ­ Brian Chesky.
If this is the philosophy you live by then we bring to you, START­UP PITCH. This event consists of two rounds – preliminary online round and the final offline round. The theme of the event is ­ “Sensor Gadgets”. Send in your start­up ideas related to this theme and we will choose the top 10 and call them on the final day to present their ideas in front of the jury. If you have a great idea then this is the chance to get rewarded for it.

CRUSADE

“I don't need to get a life. I am a gamer. I have many.”
If you get an adrenaline rush whenever you hear about NFS, Counter strike or other trending computer games, then we bring to you CRUSADE, a stand­alone gaming event. The objective is simple­ get the highest score; but as the gamers will agree, it is not as simple as it seems. Register yourself in this event and start honing your skills to ensure that you take home the crown of the ultimate gamer with you.

CODING BUZZ

“The computer programmer is a creator of universes for which he alone is responsible.” ­ Joseph Weizenbaum.
If coding problems challenge you, if removing errors from a 1000 line code enchants you, coding buzz will be your heaven. With different rounds to test all your coding skills, it is a fight of the fastest and the most accurate. One thing we can assure you of is, this event won't be as simple as solving a problem statement.

Ps, I LOVE YOU:

If this statement doesn't only remind you of the movie but also reminds you of PhotoShop (Ps),
then we have an event just for you. The start­up idea will be provided, you just have to use your superlative photoshop skills to design a poster for it. The poster must include a logo and other crucial details of the start­up company. This will be an online event and the result will be announced on Orrey.

INSIGHT – Life in Miranda

“Don't shoot what it looks like. Shoot what it feels like.”
After so many movies and ad films being shooted in this prestigious campus, we invite you to do the same. We give you one day to display your peerless videography skills. Your video should be captured in Miranda House on the day of Orrey and must be based on “Life in Miranda”. The results will be declared that very day.

What Is Orrey?


Orrey, the Tech Fest of Miranda House is a commemorative event to celebrate technology. Organised every year by the Computer Science department of Miranda House, the fest showcases, and acknowledges innovations and forthcoming opportunities in the field. Orrey 2016 will be, as per tradition, an exhibition of plethora of young talent and enthusiasm in technology along with being a festival filled with fun and excitement. The theme for Orrey 2016 is “Start Up. Stand Up” in sync with the Start-up India Scheme initiated by the Indian Government. The theme reflects the existing parallel interdependence of the various environments that exist in the technological world and the need to integrate these even further to improve the prevailing conditions in India. Through this theme, we have tried to develop an insight into the real world situation which includes a say from the political, technological, business and social environment.

TIDBITS

“My minds rebels at stagnation. I crave for mental exaltation” ­ Sherlock Holmes.

If this quote describes your frame of mind then TIDBITS will surely be your ultimate event destination. With a total of three enthralling rounds sure to test the ability of every nerve cell in your brain, it will also put on trial your team work. A maximum of four members per team are allowed and entry in the next round will be granted only if you are able to crack the previous one. The team of prodigies which is able to make it to the end will be rewarded aptly. This is an event which you will regret missing.

LET'S START-UP

“Build something 100 people love and not something one million people kind of like” ­ Brian Chesky.

If this is the philosophy you live by then we bring to you, LET'S START­UP! . This event consists of two rounds – preliminary online round and the final offline round. Come up with an innovative and unique start-up idea! You can think upon industries like Printing, Healthcare, Food and Ethnic to mention a few. Also, you have all the freedom to choose any other such field to portray your creativity. Send in your start­up ideas related to the theme of your choice and we will choose the top 10 and call them on the final day to present their ideas in front of the jury. If you have a great idea then this is the chance to get rewarded for it.

Bored of the conventional regime of coding competitions? Then we bring to you, Hunger Code.

Not to forget, code quickly or bear with your friend while he pukes on his way home because he ate too many bananas. The event requires a team of two members, one for coding and one for eating.
